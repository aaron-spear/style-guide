Fonts

SpotXchange fonts are simple, clean and should be used consistently through all materials. This will help deliver the core copy message clearly and effectively. Fonts should be SpotXchange blue for headers, and grey or black for text.

Gotham: Light, Book, Medium, Bold

Download Gotham

Uses: Main corporate font to be used in all marketing materials including signage, collateral, business cards and presentations

Open Sans: Light, Regular, Semibold, Bold

Download Open Sans

Uses: Online web-safe font for headlines, subheads, titles and body copy

Sizes

All HTML headings, <h1> through <h6>, are available. .h1 through .h6 classes are also available, for when you want to match the font styling of a heading but still want your text to be displayed inline.
